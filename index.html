About Us

Pioneering Biophysics & Biomedical Engineering Research

The Biophysics & Biomedical Engineering (BPBME) Research Group at IIT Indore is dedicated to advancing fundamental and applied research at the interface of physics, biology, and engineering. Our work focuses on developing novel diagnostic and therapeutic tools using light-matter interactions, engineered biomaterials, and advanced fabrication techniques.

We are committed to training the next generation of interdisciplinary scientists and engineers who will drive innovation in biomedical research and healthcare technology.
